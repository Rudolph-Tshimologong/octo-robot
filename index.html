ABOUT

Our Software Development Internship Programmes addresses the challenge of graduates struggling to find jobs in software development. Research has shown that graduates do not have sufficient knowledge or skills to enter the working environment and become productive in a relatively short period. At the same time, employers prefer to wait for developers with at least 2 to 4 years’ experience before they would be considered for job placement. In the meantime, the industry continues to outsource beyond our shores. Software Development Internships therefore offer an opportunity for candidates are competing to get selected and that is recognised by employers as a source of quality candidates. This programme is aiming to achieve 100% employment using the Tshimologong networks
